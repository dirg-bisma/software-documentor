# PRD Standard

## Purpose
Define *what* product should be built to solve the business problems defined in the BRD.

## Must Answer:
- Who are the target users?
- What product problem is being solved?
- What product capabilities are required?
- What features are included?
- What is the Minimum Viable Product (MVP)?
- What is the future scope?
- How will product success be measured?

## Must NOT Contain:
- Technical design or database schemas
- API architectures
- Deep dive functional edge cases (these go to SRS/FSD)
