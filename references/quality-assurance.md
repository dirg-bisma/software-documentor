# Quality Assurance Gates

Before finalizing any generated document, the AI must evaluate the document against these four quality gates.

## 1. COMPLETENESS
- Is the purpose clear?
- Is the scope defined (In Scope / Out of Scope)?
- Are the stakeholders identified?
- Are requirements uniquely identifiable?
- Are assumptions explicitly documented?
- Are open questions explicitly documented?
- Are dependencies documented?
- Are risks documented?

## 2. CONSISTENCY
- Are terms consistent across the document (and across previous documents)?
- Are roles consistent?
- Are module names consistent?
- Are statuses/states consistent?
- Are IDs unique and strictly following the standards?
- Are there any internal contradictions?

## 3. TRACEABILITY
- Can every requirement be traced backward to a business/product goal?
- Can every requirement be traced forward (if applicable)?
- Are there any "orphan" requirements?

## 4. TESTABILITY
- Is the expected behavior clear?
- Is the trigger clear?
- Is the expected output clear?
- Are exceptions and edge cases defined?
- Are the acceptance criteria testable and objective?
