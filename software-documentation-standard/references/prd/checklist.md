# PRD Checklist

- [ ] Are target user personas clearly defined?
- [ ] Are product features clearly mapped to business requirements from the BRD?
- [ ] Is the MVP clearly separated from future scope?
- [ ] Are product success metrics (KPIs) defined?
- [ ] Are requirements uniquely identifiable (e.g., FEAT-001, US-001)?
- [ ] Are assumptions and open questions explicitly stated?
- [ ] Does the document avoid dictating the technical architecture?
