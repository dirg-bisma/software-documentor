# BRD Standard

## Purpose
Define *why* the business needs a solution. Focus solely on business problems, objectives, and outcomes.

## Must Answer:
- What business problem exists?
- Why is the problem important?
- Who is affected (Stakeholders)?
- What business objective must be achieved?
- What is the business scope?
- What business outcome is expected?

## Must NOT Contain:
- API specifications
- Database schemas
- Source code
- Infrastructure configuration
- Detailed UI behavior
- Technical solutions
