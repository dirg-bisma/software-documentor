# BRD Checklist

- [ ] Does the document clearly state the business problem?
- [ ] Are the business objectives measurable?
- [ ] Are all key stakeholders identified?
- [ ] Is the scope clearly defined (In Scope and Out of Scope)?
- [ ] Are business risks identified?
- [ ] Are there no technical specifications or UI designs included?
- [ ] Are all requirements uniquely identifiable (e.g., BR-001)?
- [ ] Are assumptions and open questions explicitly stated?
