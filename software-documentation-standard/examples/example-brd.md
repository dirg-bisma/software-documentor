# 1. Executive Summary
The company currently operates 8 hotels using fragmented, standalone legacy systems. This project will deliver a centralized platform to unify operations, enabling consolidated reporting and streamlined management across all properties.

# 2. Business Problem
Data fragmentation between the 8 properties prevents corporate management from having real-time visibility into financial and operational metrics. This leads to inefficiencies in inventory management and inconsistent guest experiences.

# 3. Business Objectives
- **BR-001:** Unify operational data for all 8 properties into a single platform to enable real-time consolidated financial reporting.
- **BR-002:** Standardize the reservation and check-in processes across all properties to reduce staff training time by 20%.

# 4. Scope
## 4.1 In Scope
- Core reservation management.
- Multi-property data isolation and consolidated corporate reporting.
- Standardized Front Office workflows.

## 4.2 Out of Scope
- Restaurant/Point of Sale (POS) systems are explicitly out of scope for Phase 1.

# 5. Stakeholders
- **Corporate Operations Director:** Approves standardized workflows.
- **Hotel General Managers:** Provide property-level requirements.

# 6. Assumptions & Open Questions
- [ASSUMPTION] The legacy system vendors will provide data exports in CSV format for migration.
- [OPEN QUESTION] Will the centralized platform require integration with the existing on-premise accounting software?
